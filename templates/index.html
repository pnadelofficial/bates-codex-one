Welcome!

This site archives the first codicological and paelographical analysis of the manuscript known as Bates Code One, a mid-14th century paper manuscript, held in the Muskie Archives at Bates College.

I am Peter Nadel and currently I work at Tufts University as the Digital Humanities Natural Language Processing Specialist. But before this, I was a student at Bates, interested in Medieval manuscripts. With the support of Professor Emeritus Robert Allison, I was able to lead the first analysis of a Medieval manuscript held by the Muskie Archives. This experience was invaluable to me and I sought to preserve the work that we did together in a publically accessible website.
